Your HTML is sanitized for security. Some elements may be removed or modified.

Forbidden tags (will be removed):

  • <iframe>, <object>, <embed>, <frame>, <frameset>, <svg>, <meta>, <title>, <base>, <applet>, <source>

Limits:

  • Max 500 elements per page
  • Max 3 stylesheets (<link rel="stylesheet">)
  • Max 3 relative scripts (<script src="/...">)

Other restrictions:

  • No external content. Everything must be uploaded to your files.
  • No inline event handlers (onclick, onload, etc.)
  • No autoplay on video/audio
  • CSS @import is removed. var(--...) is not supported.
  • Form actions can't target this domain

Did you know? It's possible to have multiple pages on your tile! Just use the <a href="/second.html"></a> tag.
