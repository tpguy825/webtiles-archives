What are WebTiles?

You probably know what WebTiles are if you've stumbled all the way here, but essentially, WebTiles is a 250x250 grid of tiny websites in a tile. People with websites can claim their own tile and put whatever they want in it!

For NCArchives' tile, it's a small "brochure"-like info webtile.

What is NCArchives?

Neon City Archives (NCArchives, or NCA for short) is a website that is dedicated to Miis, and is an archive for the webmaster's Miitopia fanfiction and its universe.

Granted, at the time of writing, the website is still a work-in-progress with lots of content coming down the line. NCArchives is a passion project, a journey to create, and of course, it is made by a Mii fan, for Mii fans.

If you want to check out the website for more information and browse its contents, click here!

Why Webdev?

Making and maintaining a website is great! It's really fun and easy to make your own website. If you haven't already, why not consider making a one? There are many resources out there that teaches you the basics for free. Such as W3Schools, or MDN Web Docs!
