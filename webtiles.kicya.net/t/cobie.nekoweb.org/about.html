About Me

My name is Cobie, I am 20 years old, and I am the sole webmaster and creator of Cobiesite and all creative content within.

I exist as a goth who writes gothic fiction, but I also like to draw and review video media (I also take requests for things to review). I suffer from extreme paranoia off and on, yet miraculously am able to write the most vile and horrific things known to man. The haunted writer and all that, I guess. (I think it means I automatically write good horror like a tortured poet lol)

If you want a more serious bio, I can say that I'm a genderless, aromantic-asexual, sapphic author who never really takes anything as seriously as I should. I created the website to have an outlet for myself and my works where I wouldn't be judged by the weak minded in my abstract idea of the waking world.
